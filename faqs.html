Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I exercise?

It is recommended to exercise at least 4-5 times a week for 30-60 minutes, depending on your fitness goals.

2. What type of diet should I follow for weight loss?

A diet rich in protein, whole grains, and vegetables while reducing sugar and processed foods can help with weight loss. Consult a dietitian for personalized plans.

3. Is cardio better than weight training?

Both have unique benefits. Cardio improves heart health and burns calories, while weight training builds muscle and boosts metabolism. A mix of both is ideal.

4. How do I stay consistent with my fitness routine?

Set realistic goals, track your progress, and find a workout you enjoy. Having a workout buddy can also help with motivation.

5. Do I need supplements to achieve my fitness goals?

Supplements can help fill nutritional gaps, but they are not mandatory. A balanced diet should provide most of the nutrients you need.
